Yanlei Feng

Postdoc Researcher on climate impact science and climate solutions

Research interests lie in the intersection of forest ecology and atmosphere science.

As a researcher in earth science, Dr. Feng is deeply concerned about climate change and its impacts on the earth and human. Her research focuses on analyzing natural disturbance including hurricanes, fire, and drought using Google Earth Engine remote sensing, deep learning, and statistical tools. She is excited about the opportunities to build models to study the impacts of natural disasters on the forest ecosystem under a climate change situation. Years of research experience in climate change impacts transit her interests to actionable climate solutions. She has worked on bioenergy with carbon capture and storage, and she is working on nature-based solutions.
